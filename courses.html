Yoga for Asthma Relief

This course introduces gentle yoga poses and breathing techniques specifically designed to help manage asthma symptoms, improve lung capacity, and reduce stress.

Benefits:

  • Reduced frequency of asthma attacks
  • Stress reduction and mental calmness
  • Increased flexibility and strength
